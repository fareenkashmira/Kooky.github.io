Hello, I'm Fareen

The creative director of Kooky, I bring my skills in graphic design and psychology to the table, along with a deep sense of authenticity. My designs aim to tell stories and evoke emotions that connect with people. My work reflects creativity, insight, and precision, honed through various mediums.

I approach each project with an open and honest perspective, focusing on capturing the essence of what matters to my clients. My understanding of emotional depth comes from my background in psychology and a commitment to staying true to my values.
